New Year's Resolutions

Updated 2025-01-20 @ 17:56

I've never really been one for New Year's resolutions. Fundamentally, I've always found the whole thing arbitrary, futile, and a bit shit. Do I really back myself to be able to speak French this time next year? However, I am one for disparate and often frantic acts of life admin and abortive self-betterment, usually in the small hours and located entirely in my notes app.

It just so happens that my latest bout of auto-reinvention has fallen in the first month of the year, which means that for all intents and purposes I have created a list of New Year's resolutions.

  1. Finally get off all forms of social media (read: Instagram);
  2. See Julian Clary in something;
  3. Play the fool;
  4. Write something from start to finish;
  5. Learn to birdwatch;
  6. Get better at bass;
  7. Study magick;
  8. Improve my sleep schedule
  9. Ponder mine orb
  10. Read 10 fiction books and 10 nonfiction books
